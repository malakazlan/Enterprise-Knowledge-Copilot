# Meridian Dynamics — Onboarding Guide

## Before day one

A signed NDA is required before the first working day. The equipment order is
placed at offer acceptance; laptops arrive within 5 business days.

## First week

Every new hire is assigned an onboarding buddy during week 1. The buddy is a
peer from the same team, not the manager.

## Goals

New hires agree on 30-60-90 day goals with their manager by the end of the
second week. The 90-day review doubles as the probation review input.

## Access

System access follows least privilege: engineers receive repository and
staging access on day 1, production access only after the security training
is completed.
