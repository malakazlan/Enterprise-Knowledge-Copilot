# Meridian Dynamics — Product FAQ

## Where is customer data stored?

All customer data is stored in the EU (Frankfurt region). Data never leaves
the EU without explicit written customer consent.

## How is data encrypted?

Data is encrypted with AES-256 at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit.

## Can we export our data?

Yes. Full exports are available in JSON and CSV formats from the admin
console, self-service, at any time.

## How long is data retained?

Deleted records are retained for 90 days by default before permanent purge.
The retention window is configurable per workspace between 30 and 365 days.
