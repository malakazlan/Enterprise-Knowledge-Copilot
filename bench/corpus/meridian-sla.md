# Meridian Dynamics — Service Level Agreement (v3)

## Availability

The platform commits to 99.95% monthly uptime for all paid plans.

## Service credits

If monthly uptime falls below 99.95%, customers receive a 10% service credit.
Below 99.0%, the credit rises to 25% of the monthly fee. Credits must be
claimed within 30 days of the affected month.

## Support response times

Priority 1 (production down): first response within 15 minutes, 24/7.
Priority 2 (degraded): first response within 4 business hours.
Priority 3 (question): first response within 2 business days.

## Maintenance

Scheduled maintenance occurs Sundays between 01:00 and 03:00 UTC and is
announced at least 72 hours in advance. Scheduled maintenance inside the
announced window is excluded from uptime calculations.
