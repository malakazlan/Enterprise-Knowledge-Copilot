# Meridian Dynamics — Employee Handbook

## Time off

Full-time employees accrue 24 days of paid vacation per year, plus public
holidays in their country of employment. Unused days roll over up to a maximum
of 5 days into the next calendar year.

## Probation

New hires complete a probation period of 3 months. A review meeting with the
direct manager takes place in the final week of probation.

## Remote work

Meridian operates hybrid-first: employees may work remotely up to 3 days per
week. Fully remote arrangements require written approval from the department
head.

## Expenses

Meal expenses during business travel are reimbursed up to 45 euros per person
per day, receipts required. Alcohol is not reimbursable.
